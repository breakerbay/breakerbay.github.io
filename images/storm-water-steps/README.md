Storm Water Steps
====================================================================
Steps to go up the northern side of the block wall of the studio garage.
Thses steps will connect with the wooden steps already in place a couple of metres above the top of the retaining wall.
The steps should be slightly above the ground level on the northern side.
The storm water pipe should still be accessible.
