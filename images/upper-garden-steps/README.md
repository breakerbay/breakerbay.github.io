Upper Garden Steps
====================================================================
The Upper Garden is the area above the wooden retaining wall at the back of the studio.
Currently there is no easy way to get up to it.
The existing access via the rough steps leading up around the side of the end of the retaining wall limits access to the physically agile.
The steps should have as low as possible.
To help make this possible the lower steps will start on the (still to be built) Upper Garden Steps Deck, going up the inner side of the retaining wall, doing a 180 degree turn at the end of the wall and going up the outside of the wall to the top of the retaingin.
The Upper Garden Steps will meet with and be consistent with the Upper Garden Railing and Fence.
