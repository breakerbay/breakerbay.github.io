Upper Garden Steps Deck
====================================================================
The Upper Garden is the area above the wooden retaining wall at the back of the studio.
The Upper Garden Steps Deck is a wooden deck (or a paved area) that will connect the following areas:
* The Upper Garden Steps
* The wooden walk way behind the studio, running between the studio and the wooden retaining wall.
* The wooden steps coming up from the laundry and workshop.
